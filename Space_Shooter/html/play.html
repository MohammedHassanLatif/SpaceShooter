How to Play

Use the mouse to navigate the menus. Player 1 uses the W, A, S, D keys to move and Space-bar to attack in the direction they are facing. Player 2 uses the Arrow keys to move, NumPad 0 to attack. You must defeat the Romulan Republican Force enemies and get to Romulus to broker the treaty to bring peace between the Federation and the Romulan Empire!
