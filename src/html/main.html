Abstract of game

You play the role of Jean-Luc Picard, a young space captain on a mission to become captain of the galaxy-class USS Enterprise-D. In this game you are in control of the stargazer, after your captain was killed on the bridge. Your mission is to captain the Stargazer and its crew, to negotiate a peace truce with the Cardassians and the Federation
